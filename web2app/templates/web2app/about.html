Favourite Web series is a website app that allows a user to download the web series or movie in just one tap.


Here are some simple steps to download your Favourite web series :


* Firstly you have to find your Favourite content on Favourite Web series website. You can search manually or search in Favourite Web series Search Bar.

* Click on your Favourite web series or movie.

* Wait for few seconds. Then your movie or web series will play online.(You can also download it).
